WEB WAVE

 

Join us for an exhilarating hackathon dedicated to crafting a cutting-edge website that embodies the very essence of Openlake! This immersive event invites you to unleash your creativity and technical prowess as we strive to develop a user-friendly, lightning-fast, and visually stunning website. Participants must form teams consisting of 1-3 members for the competition. The competition is structured into two rounds, with each team undergoing evaluation at the conclusion of each round.

Apply Now
 

1

Proposal Round

1 July 2023

 

The hackathon commences with an exciting proposal round, wherein each team is required to submit a comprehensive proposal comprising two essential elements: a detailed outline of their chosen tech stack, and the rationale behind selecting that particular tech stack for achieving optimal results.

Additionally, your proposal should include well-crafted plans accompanied by snapshots of the planned user interface (UI), demonstrating the envisioned design and layout.

2

Code Round

4 July 2023

 

The exhilarating final round sets the stage for teams to bring their design to life and transform it into a fully functional website.

The challenge is to translate the visual elements and user interface into a seamless and interactive website that encompasses all the required functionality.

3

Mid-Evaluation

9 July 2023

 

During this mid-evaluation, teams will have the chance to showcase their work thus far and receive constructive input from the judges.

The feedback received during the mid-evaluation can be instrumental in refining the website and enhancing its overall quality.

4

Code Submission

14 July 2023

 

As the coding round draws to a close, participants will be asked to submit their completed websites by uploading them onto platforms such as Vercel, Netlify, or other similar hosting services.
